Who I Am

Electrical Engineering student, embedded systems builder, and small business owner.

I'm a second-year Electrical Engineering student at Florida A&M University with a 3.31 GPA, and I build real things — from embedded control systems to full-stack web applications to a solar-powered Bluetooth speaker I engineered from scratch.

I work across hardware and software. On the hardware side I'm comfortable with circuit analysis, Arduino/embedded C, PID control systems, and MATLAB/Simulink modeling. On the software side I write in Python, C++, and JavaScript, and I've built web solutions for real commercial clients.

Outside of class I run Cam's Cellular Care, LLC, a device repair and web development business I've operated since July 2020. I'm also an active member of the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) and the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE).
