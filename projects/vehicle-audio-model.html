Personal Project · MATLAB / Simulink

Electrical Modeling of My
Vehicle's Audio System

Complete March 2026
MATLABSimulinkDSPCircuit AnalysisPower Electronics

Overview

Recreated my own vehicle's car-audio power and signal architecture entirely in MATLAB/Simulink — a full electrical model built to understand and predict how the real system behaves under load, rather than just wiring it up and hoping for the best.

Highlights

Real-World Power Architecture Modeled DC distribution, grounding, and fuse protection to reflect the actual electrical path from the vehicle's battery to each audio component.
Signal Routing Simulated RCA routing and multi-channel amplification to trace how the audio signal moves and degrades through the system.
Component-Level Modeling Built detailed amplifier, subwoofer, and full-range speaker models using circuit analysis and control techniques.
DSP Evaluation Applied DSP principles to evaluate realistic loading, filtering, and distortion behavior across the modeled signal chain.
