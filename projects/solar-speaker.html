Personal Project · Hardware Integration

Solar Powered
Portable Speaker

Complete July 2024
Power ElectronicsHardware IntegrationBluetooth

Overview

Engineered and assembled a high-output solar-powered Bluetooth speaker by integrating a 12V battery, solar panel, and charging station into a custom-modified cooler housing — a ground-up power electronics build, not just an off-the-shelf kit.

Highlights

Solar Charging System Integrated a 12V battery, solar panel, and charging station to keep the speaker self-sufficient in the field.
Custom Enclosure Modified a cooler housing to hold and protect all electronics while keeping the build portable and durable.
Full Audio Chain Integration Connected a Bluetooth amplifier, subwoofer, and car speakers into a single cohesive audio system.
Power Management Used a 3-prong switch for efficient power management and signal distribution across the system.
