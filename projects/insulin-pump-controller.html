Insulin Pump Controller
using PID Control

Complete February 2026
Arduino UnoPID ControlEmbedded CTeam of 4

Overview

Collaborated in a team of four as the Electrical Engineer on a multidisciplinary team to design and prototype a closed-loop insulin pump controller using an Arduino Uno to automate glucose delivery — a project where getting the control loop wrong has real safety consequences, not just a missed spec.

Highlights

Closed-Loop Control Designed and prototyped the full closed-loop system responsible for automating glucose delivery based on continuous sensor feedback.
PID Tuning Optimized the PID control system through rigorous testing of proportional, integral, and derivative gains to reach a target glucose setpoint of 70 mg/dL.
Zero Overshoot, By Design Achieved 0% overshoot on the target setpoint — a deliberate tuning priority given the patient-safety implications of overdelivery.
Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration Worked alongside teammates from other disciplines, owning the electrical and control-systems side of a shared hardware/software deliverable.
